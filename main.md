# Welcome to PES University RR Campus Boys' Hostel Guide

## The process
1. After finishing counselling, immediately go for document submission (this determines how high up you will be on the priority list for permanent hostel assignment)
2. Go to the boys' hostel warden and inquire about rooms, they will take your contact information and will provide information at a later stage.
3. Wait for hostel orientation (this generally happens before non BTech courses start and is subject to change). 
4. Once you receive a message from the Warden office, you'll be asked to attend the orientation session on campus. Here they just yap about the rules, the messes and the blocks (I highly recommend you sleep during this stage as the coming stages are hectic)

> [!NOTE] Your parent, a local guardian and you need to be present during orientation. Without any, hostel will NOT be provided (idk why)

5. On reaching the campus, you'll be asked to provide proof that you have received a message (just show them the WhatsApp message sent by the warden. I recommend starring the message that was sent so that you can save it for future purposes), then you will receive a token. Keep it safe.
6. After the orientation, you'll be provided with:
    - 3 hostel rule books (1 for you, 1 for parent and 1 for Local Guardian)
    - 1 LG/P/O Diary
    - Hostel leaving form booklet
    - A form for filling in some details including payment details
7. After filling the form hand it over to the person in charge and they will provide you with a token. According to the token, head to the Hostel study area (signs will be posted). 
8. When your token number is called, submit the required documents, Demand Draft and a photograph. Your hostel confirmation sheet will be given.

---

## Day of Moving
1. The day of moving will be intimated by mail. The mail tells you when the orientation is and will tell you to move in 3 days prior to the day of orientation.
2. Once you reach the hostel, go check your details posted on the notice board and note down your serial number. You'll be asked to produce your Hostel confirmation, LG/P/O Diary, A passport size photo and locks. You'll be given a temporary ID card, fill in the details given and head to the place where hostel allotment is happening.
3. Tell the incharge your serial number, and give them the documents required. 
4. Once your hostel is alotted, you'll be given
    - A laundry bag with name cards and bill book
    - Coupons for your mess

5. Now head over to your hostel room and you can start putting in your things. Matresses will be sold on the day of moving and you can buy them based on which block you plan on staying in.

## Messes and Places to Eat
I will keep this brief
### Messes
1. SKM & SKM Bakehouse: Provide South Indian meals and is non veg inclusive; have the cleanest mess; food is okayish, wouldn't recommend the non-veg; Bakehouse is like a small shop to buy snacks and other bakery items like puffs, samosa etc.
2. Namdhari Foods: Provide North Indian meals and only vegetarian options; mess is alright, however it feel like eating in a dungeon; food is some of the best veg food I've eaten
3. Food Court (not to be confused with the GJBC Food Court): Provide all sorts of meals; mess is by far the most unhygenic (you will find mice); non veg inclusive; food tastes great here tho.

### Other places to eat
1. Justvend Vending Machine in the Hostel: This is for the late night cravings and to fuel late night studies
2. BE Block Tuck Shops: Has 4 shops, one sells Shawarma only, and the other 3 sell, snacks like our very famous Bun Samosa etc.
3. GJBC Food Court: Only open for Breakfast and Lunch, but they serve North Indian, South Indian, Continental etc.
4. Last but definitely not the least - Sutta Gully: The haven for all hostel students when food is shit in their messes, many many stalls that offer momos, shwarma, wraps, dosas, etc. (I would adivse on eating a little less here tho.)

 > You can change your mess every month via the SpaceBasic App. You will get login details withing 2-3 weeks after joining.
